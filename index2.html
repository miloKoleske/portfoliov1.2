Over the past years I have developed my skills in communication, advertising, social media marketing, graphic design, and web development. I've been an artist since I was 9 years old and thought that with only a little practice, I could be a better author than JK Rowling, and boy has time vindicated me.

The best thing I have ever done for myself is never put my pen down. I'm a dedicated, persistent little dude. But time has whittled down my sigma grind mindset and now I have decided that I owe it to myself to take care of my brain.

That has manifested itself in some odd ways, well, my artistic mentality, and probably the fact that I see a future ahead of me that is worth fighting for. And re:being a dedicated, persistent little dude; I want to go into a higher paying field with better benefits where I can ensure my artistic integrity in my own time. Also because my two best friends work at local labs and make hella dough and I am a jealous person and I want a garden and to raise bees.
